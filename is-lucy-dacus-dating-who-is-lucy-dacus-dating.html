After receiving a lot of positive feedback, Matador Records decided to reissue the album in September 2016. Dacus’ second album, “Historian,” was widely praised when it was released in 2018. She reteamed with Collin Pastore and Jacob Blizard in Nashville to record it. She formed the group boygenius with Phoebe Bridgers and Julien Baker in addition to her solo endeavors.

They went on a successful tour and published an EP. Over the years, Dacus released singles and covers while continuing to create music that resonated with listeners.

Her third studio album, “Home Video,” which was published in 2021, demonstrated her depth and thoughtfulness as a songwriter. On The Late Show with Stephen Colbert, she sang “Hot & Heavy,” one of the album’s songs.

In 2023, Dacus and boygenius released their eagerly awaited full-length album, “The Record.” Dacus also continued to collaborate with boygenius. But the band declared in February 2024 that they would be taking a sabbatical.
