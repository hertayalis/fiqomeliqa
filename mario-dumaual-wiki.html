Mario Dumaual Career

Mario Dumaual was the showbiz reporter who adapted to the evolution of entertainment journalism from print to TV to social media.

He helped pioneer how entertainment news transitioned from talk shows and tabloids to primetime television, since its inception in 1987.

In the early years of “TV Patrol,” Mario worked as a reporter and writer-producer of its hit segment “Star News” anchored by Angelique Lazo.
