Brad Pitt Shares Details on His Very First Kiss

September 2024 · 2 minute read
  • Brad Pitt reminisced on his first kiss during a recent interview with W Magazine.
  • It took place while he was in fourth grade, and he "ran home afterward."
  • Pitt is nominated for Best Supporting Actor at this weekend's 2020 Golden Globes and will also be a presenter at the awards ceremony.

Brad Pitt has been romantically linked to an impressive list of leading ladies throughout his decades long career, but that doesn’t mean the Once Upon a Time ... in Hollywood star doesn’t remember the first kiss that started it all.

As a part of W Magazine’s Best Performances of 2020 feature, Pitt recalled all of the adorable details of his first kiss.

“Her name was Lisa. It was in her garage. Fourth grade,” he said. “She was one street over, and I ran home afterward. I was pretty excited—the anticipation was a bit nerve-wracking. A few kids were already in on it.”

Amy Sussman//Getty Images

Pitt is expected to attend this weekend’s 2020 Golden Globes ceremony where he is nominated for Best Supporting Actor and will be a presenter for the evening, along with his ex-wife Jennifer Aniston.

In the midst of press coverage for his most recent films, Once Upon a Time ... in Hollywood and Ad Astra, Pitt has also found himself constantly denying nonstop rumors of women he’s been supposedly paired with since splitting from his wife Angelina Jolie in 2016.

“I don’t know how many women they’ve said I’ve been dating the last two or three years, and none of it’s true,” the actor told The New York Times. “I just flashed on something, but maybe it doesn’t mean anything."
