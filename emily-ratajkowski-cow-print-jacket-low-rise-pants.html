Emily Ratajkowski Pairs a Cow-Print Jacket With Low-Rise Pants

June 2024 · 2 minute read

Emily Ratajkowski embodied a forest fairy in her latest TikTok video.

The model shared a clip of herself exploring a secluded, forestlike garden while wearing a unique outdoorsy outfit. The focus point of her look was a furry black-and-white cow-print jacket with a collar and silver buttons. She layered the standout piece over a top in an abstract beige-and-brown striped print, and for bottoms, she opted for army-green low-rise denim pants cinched with a black leather belt with a large golden buckle. EmRata finished the look with her go-to blue Salomon sneakers, big gold hoops, and a dainty gold necklace.

She wore rosy makeup, and her fiery red hair fell straight down on her shoulders, contrasting the green of her surroundings.

The TikTok video was set to a trending “Fairies” sound.

She also shared a photo of the ensemble on her Instagram Story.

courtesy of Emily Ratajkowski via Instagram

If there’s anything we’ve learned about Ratajkowski’s street style, it’s that it has range.

Over the weekend, the My Body author stepped out in New York City in a skintight black minidress, which she accessorized with a low-slung belt with a gold buckle (the same one she wore in her video), and knee-high leather boots with a small heel.

And last week, she paired another pair of black leather boots—this time below-the-knee zip-ups—with a frilly white sundress by Reformation. The midi came in a painterly striped brown print and featured spaghetti straps and a leg slit. Ratajkowski accessorized with a small brown leather shoulder bag, black oval sunglasses, and her gold hoop earrings.
