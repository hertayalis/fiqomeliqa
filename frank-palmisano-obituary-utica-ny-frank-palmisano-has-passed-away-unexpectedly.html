Frank Palmisano Obituary, Death – The passing of Frank J. Palmisano, who was 73 years old at the time of his death, took place in a peaceful manner at the Wynn Hospital on Wednesday, December 13, 2023. We want to express our deepest condolences to you as we inform you of this recent development. A profound sense of loss is being felt by those who were closest to him, and they will always hold his memory in the highest regard within their hearts.

At McGrath, Myslinski, Kowalczyk & Nunn Funeral Directors, which is located at 470 French Rd. in Utica, family members and friends are cordially invited to pay their respects and express their condolences. Additionally, they are encouraged to convey their thoughts and feelings. An evening visitation is scheduled to take place on Sunday, December 17, 2023, from five o’clock until seven o’clock. As a result of the community members coming together for this meeting, they will have the opportunity to express their support for one another and to discuss the memories of Frank that they cherish the most.

A Mass of Christian Burial will be conducted at St. Anthony & St. Agnes Church in Utica, which can be found at 422 Tilden Avenue during the event. The celebration will begin at ten o’clock in the morning on Monday, December 18, and will continue until the end of the day. In the midst of this sorrowful occasion, there will be a space designated for reflection, prayer, and the commemoration of Frank’s life. During this difficult time, we want you to know that you are in our thoughts and that we extend our condolences to Frank’s family and friends. I pray that he is at peace for all eternity, and that the memories of his loving life that are shared with those who are grieving his passing will offer them comfort and solace.
