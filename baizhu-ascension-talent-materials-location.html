Genshin Impact Baizhu Ascension, Talent Materials, How To Get Them

September 2024 · 4 minute read

Increase Baizhu's stats in Genshin Impact by ascending him and his skills by farms ascension and talent level-up materials.

First Published June 29, 2023, 20:36

Genshin Impact 3.6 update brought Baizhu as a 5-star playable character, and he is one of the most beloved characters ever since he made his first appearance in the game. He is a Dendro character who uses his catalyst to thrash the enemies, and if you are able to grab him from his banner, you must ascend him and his skills to get the best out of him.

We have covered Baizhu's release date, abilities, and more separately, and if you want the complete list of his ascension and talent materials, you can find it below.

Where to Get Baizhu's Ascension, Talent Materials

  • Mora: You can farm it through Blossom of Wealth, completing daily commissions, events, and more.  
  • Negadus Emerald Sliver: It is dropped by Jadeplume Terrorshroom and Dendro Hypostases.
  • Violetgrass: It is found in near cliffs in Liyue.
  • Fungal Spores: It is dropped by the floating fungi.
  • Negadus Emerald Fragment: It is dropped by level 40+ Jadeplume Terrorshroom, level 40+ Dendro Hypostases, or you can craft it using Negadus Emerald Sliver.
  • Evergloom Ring: It is dropped by level 30+ Iniquitous Lustrators.
  • Luminescent Pollen: It is dropped by level 40+ floating fungi or can be crafted from Fungal Spores.
  • Negadus Emerald Chunk: It is dropped by level 60+ Jadeplume Terrorshroom, level 60+ Dendro Hypostases, or you can craft it using Negadus Emerald Fragment.
  • Crystalline Cyst Dust: It is dropped by level 60+ floating fungi or can be crafted from Luminescent Pollen.
  • Negadus Emerald Gemstone: It is dropped by level 75+ Jadeplume Terrorshroom, level 75+ Dendro Hypostases, or you can craft it using Negadus Emerald Chunk.
  • Teachings of Gold: It can be obtained by completing the Taishan Mansion on Wednesday, Saturday, and Sunday
  • Guide to Gold: It can be obtained by completing the Taishan Mansion on Wednesday, Saturday, and Sunday. It can also be crafted from the Teachings of Gold.
  • Philosophies of Gold: It can be obtained by completing the Taishan Mansion domain on Wednesday, Saturday, and Sunday. It can also be crafted from Guide to Gold.
  • Crown of Insight: It is a limited-duration event reward.
